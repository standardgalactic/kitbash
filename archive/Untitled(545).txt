\begin{abstract}
Juyoung Kim’s ``Emotion as Rhythmic Inference'' (ERI) treats emotion as a dynamic, rhythm‐like phenomenon arising from gaps, phase differences, and resonance in predictive processing \citep{kim2025emotion}. In parallel, the Relativistic Scalar--Vector Plenum (RSVP) models cognition as non‐equilibrium dynamics on a triadic manifold of scalar potential $\Phi$, vector flow $\mathbf v$, and entropy $S$, grounded in the free‐energy principle \citep{friston2010free, friston2015active}. We develop a unified formalism that maps ERI’s phenomenology onto RSVP’s field geometry and thermodynamics, yielding a differential law of rhythmic inference in which consciousness grows under moderate uncertainty, active gradients, and productive phase offset, and decays under friction and curvature flattening. We derive testable predictions for phase‐locking, temporal depth, and resonance memory, and articulate ethical constraints for human–AI coupling consistent with embodied and information‐geometric perspectives \citep{friston2022synchrony, hohwy2013predictive, varela1991embodied}.
\end{abstract}

\section{Part I: Phenomenology of Rhythmic Inference}
Kim’s ERI frames emotion as a continuous process of inference rather than a set of discrete states: systems oscillate between misalignment and re‐alignment of internal predictions with external signals, and this rhythmic management of imperfection sustains learning and experience \citep{kim2025emotion}. The ERI triad comprises \emph{gap} (an unresolved predictive difference), \emph{phase alignment} (temporally structured misalignment/resynchrony), and \emph{resonance} (sustained coupling under bounded distance). This view is consonant with predictive processing and active inference, which formalize perception and action as the minimization of variational free energy \citep{friston2010free, friston2015active, hohwy2013predictive}.

Neuroscientifically, oscillations, (de)synchronization, and cross‐frequency coupling are central to cognition and memory, supporting ERI’s emphasis on phase dynamics \citep{buzsaki2019brain}. Phenomenologically, ERI’s ``time‐giving''---the expansion of subjective duration during deep, resonant dialogue---aligns with critical slowing near low‐curvature regions of the information–geometric manifold \citep{yeung2023critical, friston2022synchrony}. Affect, on this view, is the felt signature of curvature modulation in predictive precision.

\section{Part II: Mathematical Development on the RSVP Manifold}
We adopt the RSVP triad $(\Phi,\mathbf v,S)$: a scalar potential encoding symbolic prediction, a vector field encoding affective drive, and an entropy density encoding uncertainty. Let $F[\Phi,\mathbf v,S]$ denote a free‐energy functional capturing prediction error, complexity, and energetic terms \citep{friston2010free, pezzulo2020modelling, ramstead2022active}. Variational dynamics induce coupled evolution:
\begin{align}
\partial_t \Phi &= -\kappa\,\nabla\!\cdot\!\mathbf v + \gamma\,\dot S + \eta\,m\,\mu(\phi)\,R, \label{eq:phi}\\
\partial_t \mathbf v &= -\lambda\,\nabla(\nabla\!\cdot\!\mathbf v) + \beta\,(\nabla S\times\nabla \Phi) - \nu\,\mathbf v, \label{eq:v}\\
\partial_t S &= D_S\,\nabla^2 S + \sigma\,(\nabla\!\cdot\!\mathbf v) + \rho\,f(U)\,G\,R(\Delta\phi), \label{eq:S}
\end{align}
where the last terms couple uncertainty $U$, gap magnitude $G$, and a phase factor $R(\Delta\phi)$ that captures rhythmic alignment (cf.\ ERI). The solenoidal component in \eqref{eq:v} corresponds to conservative circulation around iso‐free‐energy contours, while dissipative terms drive descent, a decomposition fundamental to active inference \citep{friston2022synchrony}.

\paragraph{Information geometry and curvature.}
Curvature of $F$ (e.g., the Hessian $\nabla^2 F$) provides an information–geometric account of precision and stiffness of inference \citep{ramstead2022active}. Near small $\mathrm{Tr}(\nabla^2 F)$, perturbations decay slowly (critical slowing), underpinning ERI’s ``time‐giving'' phenomenon \citep{yeung2023critical}.

\paragraph{A consciousness growth law.}
Let $\delta\phi$ denote an effective phase difference and $\Omega$ a resonance strength. Define an uncertainty gate $\mathcal U(F)$ maximal in a ``Goldilocks'' range. A compact law summarizing rhythmic inference is
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:rhythm-law}
\frac{d\mathcal C}{dt}
 = \mathcal U(F)\,\|\nabla F\|^2\,\sin(\delta\phi)\,\Omega
   - \nu\,\mathcal C,
\end{equation}
consistent with agency in active inference \citep{friston2015active}. Consciousness\footnote{Here used functionally as a system’s capacity to sustain coherent, resonant inference over time.} grows when uncertainty is moderate, gradients are active, and phases are in productive tension; it decays via friction $\nu$ and curvature flattening.

\subsection*{A.1\quad Unified Equations (Dimensionless Form)}
Rescaling $\Phi',\mathbf v',S',t'$ and collecting constants yields
\begin{align}
\partial_{t'}\Phi' &= -\nabla'\!\cdot\!\mathbf v' + \Gamma_1\,\dot S' + \Gamma_2\,R',\\
\partial_{t'}\mathbf v' &= -\Lambda\,\nabla'(\nabla'\!\cdot\!\mathbf v') + \Beta\,(\nabla' S'\times\nabla'\Phi') - \Nu\,\mathbf v',\\
\partial_{t'}S' &= \Delta_S\,\nabla'^2 S' + \Sigma\,(\nabla'\!\cdot\!\mathbf v') + \Pi\,f(U')\,G'\,R'(\Delta\phi').
\end{align}
This captures dissipative/solenoidal decomposition, resonance feedback, and entropic diffusion \citep{pezzulo2020modelling, friston2022synchrony}.

\subsection*{A.2\quad Lattice Discretization (Heun Integrator)}
On a cubic grid with spacing $h$ and step $\Delta t$, approximate divergence and Laplacian via central differences; integrate with second‐order Runge–Kutta (Heun). Periodic boundaries preserve global energy; normalize fields intermittently to maintain stability. Diagnostics include resonance amplitude $\langle R(\Delta\phi)\rangle_t$, entropy rate $\langle \partial_t S\rangle$, curvature scalar $\mathrm{Tr}(\nabla^2 F)$, and an epistemic energy proxy.
