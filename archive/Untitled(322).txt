<section id="overview">
  <h2>Series Overview</h2>
  <p>The Ark Anthology is a serialized metafable in which hierarchy, rebellion, cosmology, and world-geometry intertwine. The story is framed through Bruno's allegorical animal world on Noah’s Ark, where donkeys toil, foxes administer, lions rule symbolically, and Lorenzo the Llama mediates the tensions between them. Across the season, the Ark destabilizes under the weight of new ideas, suppressed rebellions, and dimensional insight.</p>
</section>

<section id="themes">
  <h2>Major Themes</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Hierarchy as geometry</li>
    <li>Mediation as structural complicity</li>
    <li>Rebellion drained by systemic curvature</li>
    <li>Closed-world cosmology versus multiplicity</li>
    <li>Narrative scripts as mechanisms of control</li>
    <li>Imagination as a world-breaking force</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section id="world">
  <h2>World Architecture</h2>
  <p>The Ark is not a mere ship; it's a constrained socio-narrative manifold whose curvature enforces hierarchy. Decks form gradient strata from donkeys up through foxes, lions, Noah, and ultimately the Bureau of Arks beyond reality. Deck 0, the hidden layer, absorbs suppressed revolt energy.</p>
</section>
