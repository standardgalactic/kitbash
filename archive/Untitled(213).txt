1. Formal Technical Version (RSVP Physics Tone)

Lamphrodynamic Self-Modulation Protocol

Let the system settle until the boundary-layer turbulence diminishes.

2. Phenomenological Version (Husserl / Varela Tone)

Attunement Through Lightness

Allow your awareness to widen until its texture becomes even.
Notice where experience thickens—where the body contracts, where thought knots itself, where the emotional field grows heavy.

Invite a brightness into awareness, not as an image but as a shift in how sensation dawns.
Let this brightness settle at the threshold of the troubled region.

Gently guide the brightness through it.
As it moves, the qualities of tightness and noise ease.
The field becomes more continuous, more breathable.

Extend this same gentle brightening toward parts of your world that feel oppressive—news, obligations, unresolved memories.
This doesn’t change the world; it changes the way the world appears in experience.

Let the brightness end softly, like a lamp dimming itself.
Rest in the new configuration.

3. Clinical / Psychophysiological Version (Trauma-Informed)

Coherence Flow Technique

Start by grounding: settle your breath until your body signals safety.
Scan for areas of strain—muscles holding tension, emotions that feel too loud, thoughts that won’t settle.

Imagine directing a gentle wave of clarity into that region.
This is a regulation cue, not a literal light.

Let the wave move through the discomfort at a pace that feels safe.
If intensity rises, pause and return to grounding.
If the sensation softens, continue.

As the wave passes, the nervous system shifts from sympathetic activation toward parasympathetic rest.
Repeating this method helps reduce somatic noise, calm looping thoughts, and improve sleep.

You can also use the technique for psychological triggers: bring the clarity-wave to the thought or memory, soften its emotional charge, and step back.

Close by breathing normally and noting any changes in your state.

4. Meditative / Contemplative Version (Zen / Dzogchen Tone)

The Practice of Smoothing

Sit and let the mind fall like dust on still water.
In this quietness, notice the ripples—the stuck places, the restless waves.

Bring a soft radiance to the edge of the disturbance.
Let it enter naturally, the way dawn enters a dark room.

Do not push.
Radiance moves by itself.

Where it goes, the ripples settle.
Structures loosen.
The field clears.

Let this radiance pour gently into the difficult corners of your life, not to escape them, but to see them without distortion.

When the light is finished, do nothing.
Let things be as they now are.

5. Minimalist / Poetic Version

Light Through the Field

Be still.
Find the knot.
Invite brightness.

Let it pass through, slowly.
Noise dissolves.

Carry the same brightness
to every thought that weighs you down.

When you feel the settling,
let the light fade.
Remain.

6. Esoteric / Initiatory Version (Symbolic, Without Pseudoscience)

The Inner Lamp

Enter the stillness where your breath becomes the doorway.

Seek the place in you where heaviness hides.
Call forth the Inner Lamp—your own coherence.
Hold it near the troubled region.

Now let the Lamp move.
It threads through tension like a river through stone.
Where it passes, the old shapes fall away.

Extend the Lamp to the knots in your story, to the echoes of news and memory, to the futures that trouble your sleep.
Let each be illuminated, not changed, but seen.

When the Lamp dims, remain in the clarity it leaves behind.
