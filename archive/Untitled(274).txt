\begin{abstract}
This essay formalizes a field-theoretic interpretation of wordless and imageless cognition, integrating aphantasia, anendophasia, mimetic proxy theory, and environmental stigmergy within the Relativistic Scalar–Vector Plenum (RSVP) framework. By embedding scalar and vector entropy fields as formal cognitive operators, we propose that thought can be expressed and recorded in the absence of phenomenological imagery or verbalization. Environmental interactions act as extended memory and computational scaffolds, effectively serving as externalized cognitive substrates. The resulting model reframes aphantasia and anendophasia not as deficits but as base configurations of a unified cognitive geometry, wherein internal field dynamics and external stigmergic traces form a continuous computational manifold.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
Cognition is traditionally modeled through phenomenological substrates: visual imagery, inner speech, or semantic rehearsal. Yet empirical reports of \emph{aphantasia} (absence of voluntary mental imagery) and \emph{anendophasia} (absence of inner speech) challenge the universality of these mechanisms. This work develops a unified field-theoretic account using the Relativistic Scalar–Vector Plenum (RSVP) formalism, comprising:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \(\Phi \in C^\infty(\mathbb{M})\): scalar entropy potential, encoding semantic field intensity and coherence.
    \item \(\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}} \in \Gamma(T\mathbb{M})\): vector baryon flow, representing directed cognitive, attentional, or motor currents.
    \item \(S \in C^\infty(\mathbb{M})\): local entropy density, quantifying configurational richness and mnemonic depth.
\end{itemize}

Here, \(\mathbb{M}\) is the abstract semantic–perceptual manifold, structured by cortical and subcortical dynamics. We demonstrate that thought persists and evolves coherently even when \(\Phi\) is minimally projected into visual or auditory subspaces—i.e., when phenomenological rendering is suppressed.

\section{Wordless and Imageless Cognition}
In aphantasic individuals, subjective reports confirm the absence of visual imagery, yet complex reasoning, planning, and memory remain intact. Similarly, anendophasic cognition proceeds without internal verbalization. Within RSVP, these conditions are modeled as selective suppression of higher-order projection layers, while core field dynamics remain active.

\subsection{Phantasia and Cognitive Reading Layers}
Standard cognition employs \emph{mimetic reading layers}: perceptual simulations that project semantic gradients \(\nabla \Phi\) onto sensory manifolds via motor–affective resonance. These layers are not constitutive of thought but emergent renderings. Formally, let \(\pi_V: \mathbb{M} \to \mathbb{M}_V\) and \(\pi_A: \mathbb{M} \to \mathbb{M}_A\) be projection operators into visual and auditory subspaces. Then:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Phantasia}: \(\pi_V(\Phi) \gg 0\), with \(\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\) entraining across \(\mathbb{M}_V\).
    \item \textbf{Audiolization}: \(\pi_A(\Phi) \gg 0\), with rhythmic \(\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\) in phonological circuits.
    \item \textbf{Aphantasia/Anendophasia}: \(\pi_V(\Phi) \approx 0\), \(\pi_A(\Phi) \approx 0\), but \(\Phi|_{\mathbb{M}_\text{semantic}} \neq 0\).
\end{itemize}

Cognitive efficacy is preserved because semantic traversal depends on \(\nabla \Phi\) and \(\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\), not their perceptual projections.

\subsection{Aphantasia as Semantic Base Case}
We posit aphantasia as the \emph{base plenum state}: cognition prior to mimetic rendering. Formally:
\[
\Phi = \Phi_{\text{semantic}} + \Phi_{\text{visual}} + \Phi_{\text{auditory}} + \cdots
\]
In the aphantasic regime:
\[
\Phi_{\text{visual}} \approx 0, \quad \Phi_{\text{auditory}} \approx 0, \quad \Phi_{\text{semantic}} \gg 0
\]
while \(\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\) sustains structured manipulation of abstract relations. Entropy \(S\) evolves via:
\[
\partial_t S = -\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}} \cdot \nabla S + \alpha |\nabla \Phi|^2 + \beta \|\nabla \times \boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\|^2
\]
ensuring internal consistency without sensory rehearsal.

\section{Mimetic Proxy Theory and Embodied Resonance}
Drawing on Cox’s Mimetic Proxy Theory, we interpret imagination as covert motor simulation. Gestures, facial micro-movements, and tool interactions generate embodied vector flows \(\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}_{\text{embodied}}\) that modulate \(\Phi\) and stabilize \(S\). These pathways bypass internal rendering:

\begin{itemize}
    \item A subject solves a spatial puzzle by manipulating objects: \(\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\) is externalized, \(\Phi\) updated via haptic feedback.
    \item Procedural knowledge is encoded in motor trajectories, not visual snapshots.
\end{itemize}

This \emph{embodied stigmergy} extends the cognitive field into the environment, forming a hybrid internal–external computational loop.

\section{Midbrain Modulation and Amplitwistor Dynamics}
Midbrain central pattern generators (CPGs) impose rhythmic templates on \(\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\), inducing oscillatory synchronization across cortical columns. Define the \emph{amplitwistor mode} \(\mathcal{A}_k\) as:
\[
\mathcal{A}_k(t) = \int_{\Sigma_k} \Phi(\mathbf{x},t) e^{i \omega_k t + \mathbf{p}_k \cdot \mathbf{x}} \, d^3x
\]
where \(\Sigma_k \subset \mathbb{M}\) is a synchronized submanifold. The dynamics are:
\[
\dot{\mathcal{A}}_k = i \omega_k \mathcal{A}_k - \gamma_k \mathcal{A}_k + \sum_{i,j} \mathsf{C}_{ij}(t) \nabla_j \Phi_i + \Gamma_k
\]
with \(\mathsf{C}_{ij}(t)\) the CPG-gated coupling tensor and \(\Gamma_k\) the TARTAN–CLIO drive (see Appendix).

In aphantasia, CPG entrainment remains below threshold for sensory manifolds; \(\mathcal{A}_k\) decays rapidly in \(\mathbb{M}_V\), \(\mathbb{M}_A\), but persists in semantic coordinates.

\section{Environmental Stigmergy as Interactive Memory}
Stigmergy—coordination via environmental traces—serves as \emph{externalized cognition}. Agents inscribe \(\Phi\), \(\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\), and \(S\) into the world:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Marks as \(\Phi\)}: A drawn diagram embeds scalar semantic potential.
    \item \textbf{Sequences as \(\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\)}: Step-by-step manipulation traces directed flow.
    \item \textbf{Configurations as \(S\)}: Object arrangements encode entropy-rich states.
\end{itemize}

The environment becomes a writable/readable extension of the plenum:
\[
\mathbb{M}_{\text{total}} = \mathbb{M}_{\text{internal}} \cup \mathbb{M}_{\text{external}}, \quad \partial \mathbb{M}_{\text{int/ext}} \text{ permeable}
\]
Enabling offloading, recombination, and long-term stabilization of cognitive states.

\section{Discussion}
The RSVP formalism unifies internal and external cognitive architectures. Aphantasia and anendophasia are not deficits but \emph{purified modes} of entropic computation—direct traversal of semantic geometry without mimetic overhead. Environmental stigmergy extends this purity into the physical world, forming a continuous field of thought.

This perspective has implications for:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Neurodiversity}: Reframing non-phenomenological cognition as architectural variation.
    \item \textbf{Embodied AI}: Designing systems that compute via scalar–vector fields and environmental interaction.
    \item \textbf{Education}: Leveraging external scaffolds (diagrams, models) to support imageless learners.
\end{itemize}

Future work should simulate RSVP–TARTAN–CLIO dynamics under varying CPG gain and stigmergic feedback to predict behavioral signatures across the cognitive spectrum.

\[
\begin{aligned}
\partial_t \Phi &= -\nabla \cdot (\Phi \boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}) + \xi(t) \\
\partial_t \boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}} &= (\nabla \Phi) \times \boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}} - \gamma \boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}} + \nabla \cdot \mathsf{D} \nabla \boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}} + \boldsymbol{\eta}(t) \\
\partial_t S &= -\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}} \cdot \nabla S + \alpha \|\nabla \Phi\|^2 + \beta \|\nabla \times \boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\|^2 + \kappa \Delta S
\end{aligned}

where \(\xi, \boldsymbol{\eta}\) are stochastic midbrain inputs, \(\mathsf{D}\) is a diffusion tensor, and \(\gamma, \alpha, \beta, \kappa > 0\).
