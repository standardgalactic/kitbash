\begin{abstract}
This essay formalizes a field-theoretic interpretation of wordless and imageless cognition, integrating aphantasia, anendophasia, mimetic proxy theory, and environmental stigmergy within the Relativistic Scalar–Vector Plenum (RSVP) framework.  By embedding scalar and vector entropy fields as formal cognitive operators, we propose that thought can be expressed and recorded in the absence of phenomenological imagery or verbalization.  Environmental interactions act as extended memory and computational scaffolds, effectively serving as externalized cognitive substrates.  The resulting model reframes aphantasia and anendophasia not as deficits but as base configurations of a unified cognitive geometry, wherein internal field dynamics and external stigmergic traces form a continuous computational manifold.
\end{abstract}

% --------------------- Main Text ---------------------
\section{Introduction}
Cognition is traditionally modeled through phenomenological substrates: visual imagery, inner speech, or semantic rehearsal.  Yet empirical reports of \emph{aphantasia} (absence of voluntary mental imagery) and \emph{anendophasia} (absence of inner speech) challenge the universality of these mechanisms.  This work develops a unified field-theoretic account using the Relativistic Scalar–Vector Plenum (RSVP) formalism, comprising
\begin{itemize}
  \item $\Phi\in C^\infty(\mathbb{M})$: scalar entropy potential, encoding semantic field intensity and coherence;
  \item $\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\in\Gamma(T\mathbb{M})$: vector flow, representing directed cognitive, attentional, or motor currents;
  \item $S\in C^\infty(\mathbb{M})$: local entropy density, quantifying configurational richness and mnemonic depth.
\end{itemize}
Here $\mathbb{M}$ is the abstract semantic–perceptual manifold structured by cortical and subcortical dynamics.  Thought persists even when $\Phi$ is minimally projected into sensory subspaces—when phenomenological rendering is suppressed.

\section{Wordless and Imageless Cognition}
Aphantasic individuals lack visual imagery yet reason normally; anendophasic individuals think without internal verbalization.  In RSVP these conditions reflect suppression of higher-order projection layers while the underlying fields remain active.

\subsection{Phantasia and Cognitive Reading Layers}
Standard cognition employs \emph{mimetic reading layers}: perceptual simulations projecting semantic gradients $\nabla\Phi$ onto sensory manifolds via motor–affective resonance.  Let $\pi_V:\mathbb{M}\!\to\!\mathbb{M}_V$ and $\pi_A:\mathbb{M}\!\to\!\mathbb{M}_A$ project into visual and auditory subspaces.  
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Phantasia:} $\pi_V(\Phi)\!\gg\!0$ with $\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}$ entraining across $\mathbb{M}_V$.
  \item \textbf{Audiolization:} $\pi_A(\Phi)\!\gg\!0$ with rhythmic $\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}$ in phonological circuits.
  \item \textbf{Aphantasia/Anendophasia:} $\pi_V(\Phi)\!\approx\!0$, $\pi_A(\Phi)\!\approx\!0$, but $\Phi|_{\mathbb{M}_{\text{semantic}}}\!\neq\!0$.
\end{itemize}
Cognitive efficacy depends on $\nabla\Phi$ and $\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}$, not on their sensory projections.

\subsection{Aphantasia as Semantic Base Case}
We treat aphantasia as the \emph{base plenum state}, cognition prior to mimetic rendering:
\[
\Phi=\Phi_{\text{semantic}}+\Phi_{\text{visual}}+\Phi_{\text{auditory}}+\cdots
\]
In the aphantasic regime
\[
\Phi_{\text{visual}}\!\approx\!0,\quad
\Phi_{\text{auditory}}\!\approx\!0,\quad
\Phi_{\text{semantic}}\!\gg\!0,
\]
while $\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}$ sustains abstract manipulation.  Entropy evolves as
\[
\partial_tS=-\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\!\cdot\!\nabla S
+\alpha|\nabla\Phi|^2
+\beta\|\nabla\times\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}\|^2,
\]
ensuring internal coherence without sensory rehearsal.

\section{Mimetic Proxy Theory and Embodied Resonance}
Cox’s Mimetic Proxy Theory views imagination as covert motor simulation.  Gestures and tool interactions generate embodied vector flows $\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}_{\text{embodied}}$ that modulate $\Phi$ and stabilize $S$.  
Procedural knowledge is thus encoded in motor trajectories rather than visual or verbal icons, extending cognition into the environment—\emph{embodied stigmergy}.

\section{Midbrain Modulation and Amplitwistor Dynamics}
Midbrain CPGs impose rhythmic templates on $\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}$, inducing oscillatory synchronization across cortical columns.  Define
\[
\mathcal{A}_k(t)=\!\int_{\Sigma_k}\!\Phi(\mathbf{x},t)
e^{i\omega_kt+\mathbf{p}_k\cdot\mathbf{x}}\,d^3x,
\]
with dynamics
\[
\dot{\mathcal{A}}_k
=i\omega_k\mathcal{A}_k
-\gamma_k\mathcal{A}_k
+\sum_{i,j}\mathsf{C}_{ij}(t)\nabla_j\Phi_i
+\Gamma_k.
\]
Aphantasia corresponds to sub-threshold CPG gain: $\mathcal{A}_k$ decays in sensory manifolds but persists semantically.

\section{Environmental Stigmergy as Interactive Memory}
Stigmergy—coordination through environmental traces—externalizes cognition.
\begin{itemize}
  \item Marks ($\Phi$): diagrams embedding scalar potential.
  \item Sequences ($\boldsymbol{\mathcal{v}}$): directed manipulation traces.
  \item Configurations ($S$): entropy-rich object states.
\end{itemize}
The total manifold
\[
\mathbb{M}_{\text{total}}
=\mathbb{M}_{\text{internal}}\cup
 \mathbb{M}_{\text{external}},
\qquad
\partial\mathbb{M}_{\text{int/ext}}\text{ permeable},
\]
permits cognitive offloading and distributed computation.

\section{Discussion}
Aphantasia and anendophasia are purified modes of entropic computation—direct traversal of semantic geometry without mimetic overhead.  Environmental stigmergy extends this process into the physical world.  The model implies new directions for neurodiversity, embodied AI, and education.  Future simulations of RSVP–TARTAN–CLIO dynamics under varying CPG gain and stigmergic feedback may predict observable spectra of cognition.
