\subsection*{A.1. Manifolds and Organismic Worldtubes}

The Relativistic Scalar--Vector Plenum (RSVP) represents an organism and its
physical environment as a four–dimensional differentiable manifold
\(
\mathcal{M}
\)
equipped with local coordinates
\(
x^\mu = (t, x^1, x^2, x^3)
\).
An individual organism traces out a timelike curve
\(
\gamma: I \rightarrow \mathcal{M}
\),
and the ``worldtube'' surrounding this trajectory is an immersed submanifold
\(
\mathcal{W} \subset \mathcal{M}
\)
representing the region in which scalar, vector, and entropy fields are coupled.

The fundamental fields of RSVP are:
\[
\Phi: \mathcal{M} \rightarrow \mathbb{R},
\qquad
v^\mu: \mathcal{M} \rightarrow T\mathcal{M},
\qquad
S: \mathcal{M} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}_{\ge 0}.
\]
The scalar field \(\Phi\) captures potential-like structure; the vector field
\(v^\mu\) captures directional flux, flow, and agency; and the entropy field
\(S\) encodes coarse-grained disorder and homeostatic deviation.
Together these define a dynamical state
\(
\mathbf{Z}(x) = (\Phi, v^\mu, S)
\)
at every point \( x \in \mathcal{M} \).

The geometry of \(\mathcal{M}\) is specified by a (pseudo-)Riemannian metric
\(
g_{\mu\nu}
\),
which allows us to define norms, contractions, and differential operators such
as:
\[
\nabla_\mu v^\nu, \qquad
\square \Phi = g^{\mu\nu} \nabla_\mu \nabla_\nu \Phi.
\]
This geometric structure provides the background on which RSVP's dynamical
equations evolve.

\subsection*{A.2. Bundles, Sections, and Biological Constraints}

Organismic structure imposes additional constraints that are naturally described
using fiber bundles. For example, the internal physiological state space
\(
\mathcal{H}
\)
--- comprising hydration, glucose levels, temperature, and related
variables --- is modeled as a manifold. A section
\[
\sigma: \mathcal{W} \rightarrow \mathcal{W} \times \mathcal{H}
\]
assigns to each spacetime point a well-defined internal state.

Similarly, neural and cognitive variables are represented on vector bundles
\(
E_L \rightarrow \mathcal{W}
\)
associated with different CLIO layers \(L\in\{0,1,2,3\}\).
Each layer has a latent space:
\[
z_L(x)\in \Gamma(E_L),
\]
which evolves by minimizing a free-energy-like objective functional.
The geometry of each bundle determines:
\begin{itemize}
\item admissible directions of change,
\item coupling between levels,
\item constraints imposed by biological structure.
\end{itemize}

This bundle-theoretic formulation unifies RSVP's physical fields with CLIO's
cognitive fields.

\subsection*{A.3. Entropy, Potentials, and Viability Regions}

A central principle of RSVP is that viable organisms occupy a bounded region in
state space. Let
\[
\mathcal{V} \subset \mathcal{H}
\]
denote the viability set.
Homeostasis corresponds to maintaining the internal state within \(\mathcal{V}\)
or minimizing deviation from it.

Given a state \(h \in \mathcal{H}\), define the homeostatic deviation
\[
\Delta(h) = d_{\mathcal{H}}(h, \mathcal{V}),
\]
where
\(d_{\mathcal{H}}\)
is the geodesic distance induced by a Riemannian metric on \(\mathcal{H}\).
The entropy field \(S(x)\) is then modeled as a monotone function of
\(\Delta(h(x))\). This construction yields:
\[
S(x) = f(\Delta(h(x))),
\qquad 
f'(u) > 0.
\]

The scalar potential \(\Phi\) encodes expected energy--entropy exchange with the
environment, giving rise to gradients of the form:
\[
\nabla_\mu \Phi \sim \partial_\mu S + \text{interaction terms}.
\]
Thus RSVP casts organismic regulation as a problem in differential geometry:
balance entropy gradients against potential and flux fields.

\subsection*{A.4. The Inference Manifold and Natural Gradient Flow}

CLIO models cognition as gradient descent on a manifold of beliefs.
Let
\[
\Theta = \{ \theta \}
\]
be the parameter or latent space associated with a generative model.
Equipped with the Fisher information metric \(g_{ij}(\theta)\), the inference
space
\(
(\Theta, g)
\)
becomes a Riemannian manifold.

Define a free-energy-like functional:
\[
F(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_{q_\theta}[\log q_\theta - \log p],
\]
where \(p\) is the target distribution (or implicit generative model).

The natural gradient descent update is:
\[
\dot{\theta} = -g^{-1}(\theta)\nabla F(\theta),
\]
which generalizes to field form on \(\mathcal{W}\):
\[
\nabla_{\partial t} z_L(x)
=
-\Pi_L(x)\, g^{-1}_L(z_L(x))
\,\frac{\delta F}{\delta z_L(x)}.
\]

This expression constitutes the continuous-time limit of the CLIO update rule.
Precision weighting \(\Pi_L\) plays the role of an inverse metric deformation,
modulating the effective curvature of the inference manifold.

\subsection*{A.5. Geodesics, Curvature, and Coherence}

The coherence of a cognitive or physical system depends on the curvature
structure of its manifold. Let \(R^i_{\ jkl}\) be the Riemann curvature tensor
associated with the metric on \(\Theta\).

\begin{itemize}
\item High curvature corresponds to stiff, hard-to-update beliefs,
\item Low curvature corresponds to flexible, easily-updated beliefs.
\end{itemize}

Coherence across CLIO layers is enforced when geodesics in each layer's
manifold align under the coupling maps
\(
\varphi_{L\rightarrow L+1}: E_L \rightarrow E_{L+1}.
\)

Formally, recursive coherence requires:
\[
\nabla_{\dot{\gamma}}\dot{\gamma} = 0
\quad\text{simultaneously on all coupled manifolds } E_L.
\]

This geometric condition parallels RSVP’s requirement that physical fields
maintain a consistent entropy--potential configuration across a worldtube.

\subsection*{A.6. Field Couplings and Multi-Layer Dynamics}

Finally, we formalize the coupling between RSVP fields and CLIO fields. Let
\[
\mathbf{Z}(x) = (\Phi, v^\mu, S)
\quad\text{and}\quad
\mathbf{z}(x) = \{ z_L(x) : L=0,1,2,3 \}.
\]
We define a joint configuration bundle:
\[
E = E_{\text{phys}} \oplus \bigoplus_{L=0}^3 E_L,
\]
and a total free-energy functional:
\[
F_{\text{tot}}[\mathbf{Z}, \mathbf{z}]
=
\int_{\mathcal{W}} 
\mathcal{L}_{\text{RSVP}}(\Phi, v^\mu, S)
+
\sum_{L=0}^3 
\mathcal{L}_L(z_L, \partial z_L)
+
\mathcal{L}_{\text{int}}(\mathbf{Z},\mathbf{z}) 
\ \mathrm{d}V.
\]

The Euler--Lagrange equations on this composite bundle yield:
\[
\frac{\delta F_{\text{tot}}}{\delta \mathbf{Z}} = 0,
\qquad
\frac{\delta F_{\text{tot}}}{\delta z_L} = 0,
\]
which encode the coupled dynamics of physical fields and cognitive fields.

This unified geometric framework enables all subsequent variational, dynamical,
and computational derivations.
