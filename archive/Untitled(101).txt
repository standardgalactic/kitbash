 “Eloi versus The Morlocks”

INT. SUBTERRANEAN CHAMBER – NIGHT

CAMERA glides low across a reflective pool, revealing the ELOI—fragile, luminous beings in diaphanous garments, their skin almost translucent under the pale blue glow. They stand barefoot in the water, eyes wide, ethereal faces flickering with both fear and awakening defiance.

From the far shadows, the MORLOCKS emerge: hulking, pale-fleshed predators with wet, gleaming eyes. The sound design swells—distant machinery blending with the rhythmic panting of the Morlocks, a heartbeat of the underworld itself.

SLOW MOTION as the first Eloi steps forward, defiance etched into their fragile features. The clash is about to begin—not just a battle for survival, but a rebellion against eons of fear.
